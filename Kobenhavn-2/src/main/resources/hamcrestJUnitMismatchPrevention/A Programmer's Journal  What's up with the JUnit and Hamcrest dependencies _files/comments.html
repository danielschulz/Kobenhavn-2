I am testing with / learning  with 4.11 
4.11 does not have the hamecrest-library   -it just "has"  just the core (separate zip file on the  junit site)
so based on your notes  I got it to work   with pom having the following 
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.hamcrest</groupId>
            <artifactId>hamcrest-library</artifactId>
            <version>1.3</version>
            <scope>test</scope>
        </dependency>
        <dependency>
            <groupId>junit</groupId>
            <artifactId>junit</artifactId>
            <version>4.11</version>
            <scope>test</scope>
        </dependency>
